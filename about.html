Welcome to the Monty Tech Greenhouse website, where we list all the plants and masonry available to purchase, as well as their price and related info.



The Monty Tech greenhouse is a building behind Monty Tech for buying plants and different masonry projects made by the students in masonry.
The Monty Tech Greenhouse is located at 1050 Westminster St and is open when school is in session from 11am to 1pm starting early May.
The greenhouse is open to the public meaning anyone can come and purchase plants. You can't buy plants online so you have to
come buy them in person. The greenhouse offers a variety of plants like flowers, vegetables, hanging baskets
and other types of plants. Masonry offers different projects like sculptures of animals or pottery.
If you want to learn more about the greenhouse or masonry, you can visit the Monty Tech website here.
